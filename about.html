Our Vision

To be the foremost theological institution training Christ-like leaders and academics that will tremendously impact the world positively.

Our Mission

To expand the frontiers of knowledge in Christian religious education. To raise Christ-like graduates to employ technological and intercultural tools effectively and efficiently

Our Story

It is a long established fact that

The Redeemed Christian Bible college, being the brain-child of the present General Overseer, Pastor E.A. Adeboye was founded by the authority of the Redeemed Christian church of God in September 1980. The Church is registered under the Land (Perpetual Succession) of 1990. The College is at 1-5 Redemption Way (formerly 1a. Cemetery) Street. Ebute-Meta Lagos Nigeria.
